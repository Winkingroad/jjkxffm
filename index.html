About FFM x JJK

Cursed arenas rise as college elites battle for absolute dominance

Free Fire MAX x Jujutsu Kaisen transforms 3 cities and 15 colleges into live cursed domains where student warriors rise to prove their supremacy.

This is not just a tournament—it’s a full-scale esports invasion, featuring high-stakes competitive matches, adrenaline-fueled campus activations, and a fully immersive live anime experience inspired by the JJK universe.

Beyond the battlefield, unleash your skills through treasure hunts, quiz challenges, fun games, and iconic Jujutsu Kaisen figure challenges, turning every campus into a clash of strategy, skill, and cursed power.

Bonus Battles, Quests & JJK Challenges

Step beyond the arena and enter cursed side quests designed to test skill, speed, and strategy.

From treasure hunts and quiz challenges to fun games and iconic Jujutsu Kaisen figure challenges, each campus becomes an interactive battleground.

These live activities amplify the esports experience, blending anime fandom, competition, and high-energy campus engagement.

Cosplay showcases and influencer meet-and-greets bring the Jujutsu Kaisen universe to life, connecting fans with creators, characters, and the culture.
